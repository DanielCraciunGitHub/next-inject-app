## Why Do We Enforce This Project Structure?

This project structure is derived from the **best practices** acquired since the
[app router release](https://nextjs.org/blog/june-2023-update) in June 2023.

After 1 year of trial and error, we know what works
and what doesn't work, and we put all of our knowledge into this robust
project structure suitable for projects of all size.
